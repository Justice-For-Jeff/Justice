Verified federal case

This matter is pending in the United States District Court. The lawsuit asserts claims under the Rehabilitation Act and the Age Discrimination in Employment Act (ADEA).

The United States Postal Service denies the allegations. No findings have been made by the court.

View PACER Monitor Docket Support & Transparency

Case overview

Jeff Patterson is a former USPS City Carrier Technician proceeding pro se. After a documented spinal injury, a written modified-duty agreement reflected medical restrictions.

The operative complaint alleges that the restrictions were not honored and that assignments beyond those limits led to medically necessary absences. According to the pleadings, those absences were later cited as grounds for progressive discipline and termination.

The defendant disputes these allegations. The case remains active, and all facts will be determined by the court.
